Chocolate Date Shake

Instructions

  1. To a high-speed blender add frozen banana, cauliflower rice, pitted date, carob or cacao powder, flax, cinnamon, and almond milk and blend on high until creamy and smooth. Add more dairy-free milk or a little water as needed if it has trouble blending.
  2. Serve immediately and garnish with additional cacao nibs (optional).

Ingredients

  • 1/2 Frozen Banana (2)
  • 1 cup frozen cauliflower rice
  • 1 cup almond milk
  • 1/2 banana, frozen
  • 2 tablespoons flax seeds
  • 2 tablespoons cacao powder
  • 1/2 teaspoon cinnamon
  • 1/2 teaspoon vanilla extract
  • optional cacao nibs as a topping

Nutrition Facts

Calories: 540

Carbohydrates:38g

Sugars: 13g

Fat: 26g

Saturated Fat: 2g

Fiber: 12g

Frozen Cauliflower Rice

Benefits

Cauliflower is a cruciferous vegetable that is naturally high in fiber and B-vitamins. It provides antioxidants and phytonutrients that can protect against cancer. It also contains fiber to enhance weight loss and digestion, choline that is essential for learning and memory, and many other important nutrients.

Bananas

Benefits

Bananas are a good source of several vitamins and minerals, especially potassium, vitamin B6, and vitamin C. Potassium. Bananas are a good source of potassium. A diet high in potassium can lower blood pressure in people with elevated levels and benefits heart health.

Dates

Benefits

Medjool dates are also an excellent source of phytonutrients, plant compounds that may have health benefits. Studies have shown they can stimulate the immune system, reduce inflammation, prevent DNA damage, and improve hormone regulation.

Cacao

Benefits

The superfood of all superfoods, cacao—the dried seeds at the root of chocolate—is also one of the highest sources of magnesium in nature, full of antioxidants, calcium, zinc, copper and selenium. Cacao contains more antioxidants per gram than blueberries, goji berries, red wine, raisins, prunes and even pomegranates.

Flax Seeds

Benefits

Flaxseeds are loaded with nutrients, including omega-3 fatty acids, fiber, and beneficial plant compounds like lignans, all of which boast many potential health benefits. This tiny seed may improve digestive health, protect against certain types of cancer, stabilize blood sugar levels, and lower blood pressure and cholesterol levels.

Cinnamon

Benefits

Cinnamon contains antioxidants, which are a healthy component of the diet that help neutralize free radicals, preventing them from damaging cells in the body. Cinnamaldehyde also has some antibacterial properties and might slow down the growth of cancerous cells. Cinnamon may help lower blood sugar and fight diabetes by imitating the effects of insulin and increasing glucose transport into cells

Almond Milk

Benefits

Almond milk is rich in vitamin E, which is an important antioxidant. Vitamin E can help lower your risk of serious health conditions like stroke, heart disease, and even cancer.
